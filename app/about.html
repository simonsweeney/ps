Entry Title

The Laya Healthcare Pendulum Summit is a day-long forum for discussion that was born from the premise that everyone, from the successful businessman to the aspiring academic strives for something more,something bigger and in essence tries to find those magic ingredients which will allow them to maximise their potential in all aspects of their life.

The Laya Healthcare Pendulum Summit will showcase a diverse range of international and ‘home grown’ speakers and guests who have reached the pinnacle of their industry. They will demonstrate how they use a combination of hard work, innovation, inspiration and motivation to overcome adversity and maintain a successful, balanced life while keeping their eye on the end goal. Our speakers will be experts in business, technology, organisational development and much more. These industry leaders will be invited to dig a little deeper into the areas of psychology to demonstrate the mind-set behind their achievements. The fact that the speakers will have a diverse approach and style to such topics will make for a one of a kind event!
